FAQ

Common questions, answered.

Which versions of Excel does ZAFtool support?

ZAFtool works with Microsoft Excel 2016, 2019, 2021, and Microsoft 365 on Windows. Mac support coming soon.

How long does installation take?

Less than 5 minutes. You'll receive the .xlam file + step-by-step guide. The ZAFtool ribbon appears automatically.

Is my data safe?

100% safe. Everything runs locally inside Excel. No data leaves your computer. No internet required after installation.

Can I install on multiple computers?

One licence = one user. You can install it on up to 2 machines belonging to you personally (e.g. your work laptop + home laptop). It is not transferable to a colleague — they would need their own licence. For teams of 5+, see our Team pricing for volume discounts.

What if I don't like it?

14-day money-back guarantee. If it doesn't save you time, just ask for a full refund — no questions asked.

Do you offer team / firm pricing?

Yes! Volume discounts up to 15% for 5+ users. Click "Get Team Quote" in the pricing section above.

How exactly does the free trial work?

Enter your email on the trial page and you'll get an instant download link for the full ZAFtool .xlam file. Install it in Excel (under 5 minutes), and all 25 tools are yours to use freely for 14 days. No credit card, no WhatsApp, no manual steps. After 14 days, you can purchase the licence to keep using it.

Do I need to know VBA or macros to use ZAFtool?

Not at all. ZAFtool is a point-and-click add-in. Every tool is a single button in your ribbon. You don't write any code, record any macros, or configure anything. If you can use Excel, you can use ZAFtool from day one.

Why not just record my own macros?

You could — but recording macros takes time to build, breaks when workbook structures change, and doesn't give you intelligent tools like Model Audit (which runs 6 checks and generates a clickable error report) or Consolidate Files (which handles folders of files with different column orders). ZAFtool tools are hardened for real finance workflows, not fragile recordings.

How is ZAFtool different from Macabacus?

Macabacus is a great enterprise product at ₹16,000+/year, covering Excel, PowerPoint, and Word with 100+ features and a steep learning curve. ZAFtool is 1/6th the price, Excel-only, and laser-focused on the 25 tools Indian finance professionals actually use every day — with zero learning curve and no lag on large models (a common Macabacus complaint).
